Smart Blocker

Specify sites you want to block below. Each site must be on a separate line. Sites starting with ! will be exception to the rule, allowed.


Autofills block sites with common distractors.
Points added per minute on merit sites.
Points subtracted per minute on demerit sites.

Optional Settings

A limit to points accumulated. OPTIONAl.
Once enabled, points will reset if disabled.
